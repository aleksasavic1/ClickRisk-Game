RULES

ClickRisk is a game where 2 players compete to reach 50 points first. Each click of the button has a 50% chance of giving the player +10 points and a 50% chance of resetting their score to 0 points. Upon pressing the Confirm button, the turn is passed to the other player. If a player's score is reset to 0, the turn automatically switches to the other player.
